When given a slide deck, this skde study tool will first choose the most important slides in the slide deck and compile them into a short slidedeck that will serve as a useful summary for the slidedeck. I hope you like it. Just do this:

  1. Upload Slidedeck
  2. Download Summary
  3. Et viola! You're done.

I would like to show you a demonstration of the selection of the summarizer for a given slidedeck.

Slidedeck credit goes to the LILY group at Yale that publicly shared that slidedeck as well as manny others on its open-source "lecture bank" on Github.
